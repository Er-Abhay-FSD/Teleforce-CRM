How to Use Teleforce CRM for Effective Lead Management

A guide for telecallers to handle leads and calls efficiently

Overview of Teleforce CRM

Teleforce CRM helps manage incoming calls and direct leads effectively, ensuring seamless call disposition and automating lead status updates for a simplified workflow.

  • Simplified workflow
  • Single-click lead management
  • Automatic data syncing

Logging into Teleforce CRM

  1. Open the CRM application/website.
  2. Enter your ID and password.
  3. Access the dashboard.

Handling Leads

1. Incoming Calls:
  • Save customer contact if interested.
  • Convert into a lead by filling in:
    • Source of the lead (e.g., Social Media, Website, or Referral).
    • Disposition based on customer interaction:
      • Interested
      • Follow-Up Required
      • Not Interested
2. Direct Leads:
  • Track dispositions to update lead statuses:
    • New Lead
    • Qualified Lead
    • Sales Qualified Lead
    • Proposal Sent
    • Negotiation
    • Closed-Won
    • Closed-Lost
    • Disqualified
    • Follow-Up Needed
    • Pending
  • Appears in the Leads Panel automatically.
  • Initiate calls directly from the panel for efficiency.
  • Dispositions automatically update the lead status and are logged in the Notes section.

Call Disposition Workflow

Incoming Call → Save Contact → Convert to Lead → Add Source and Disposition → Auto-update Lead Status

Direct Lead → Make Call → Update Call Disposition → Auto-update Lead Status

What You Can Do with Teleforce CRM

  • Save contact details for incoming calls.
  • Convert leads seamlessly.
  • Call leads directly via the Leads Panel.
  • Update dispositions with automatic syncing.

Guidelines for Telecallers

  • Save contact details for all interested customers.
  • Always update disposition immediately after calls.
  • Utilize the Leads Panel for direct call leads.
  • Ensure data accuracy during lead conversion.

Benefits of Using the Workflow

  • Eliminate redundant data entry.
  • Save time with automated updates.
  • Better tracking of leads and call outcomes.
  • Improved customer experience.

Summary

Log in → Handle incoming calls and direct leads → Update dispositions → Convert leads efficiently.

CRM automates updates, reducing workload and ensuring accurate tracking.
